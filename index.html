About Us

Established in 1985, St. Andrews Matriculation School has been dedicated to shaping young minds with a holistic approach to education. Our mission is to inspire excellence and nurture growth through a balanced blend of academics, values, and extracurricular activities.

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a safe and inclusive learning environment. Join us to embark on a journey of academic excellence and character building.
