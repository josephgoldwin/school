Admission Details

St. Andrews Matriculation School offers an excellent academic environment for students. Admission is open for classes 1 to 12, based on the availability of seats.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must meet the following criteria to be eligible for admission:

  • Minimum age of 5 years for class 1 admission.
  • Completion of the previous grade for admission to higher classes.
  • Submission of required documents (birth certificate, previous school reports, etc.).

Fees Structure

Our school offers an affordable and comprehensive fee structure to accommodate all students. The fees vary based on the grade level.
